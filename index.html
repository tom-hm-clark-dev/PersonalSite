About TMC Web Design

I’m Tom Clark, a web development graduate from Aberystwyth University with a passion for crafting responsive, creative websites. After gaining hands-on experience in a global e-commerce environment, I’m now bringing my expertise directly to clients as a freelance developer.

Pre made site builders such as Wix and Squarespace come with a lot of unneccessary bulk that can slow your website down dramatically. Slow websites drive customers away. I use best web practises to program fast websites from scratch, getting you the precise design you need without any of the clutter slowing you down.
